What is NFTs ?

 
  An NFT is a unit of data, stored on a type of digital ledger called
  a blockchain, which can be sold and traded. The NFT can be associated 
  with a particular digital or physical asset such as images, art, music,
  and sports highlights and may confer licensing rights to use the 
  asset for a  specified purpose.
  

  NFTs are individual tokens with valuable information stored in
  them. Because they hold a value primarily set by the market and demand,
  they can be bought and sold just like other physical types of art.
  NFTs' unique data makes it easy to verify and validate their ownership
  and the transfer of tokens between owners.
